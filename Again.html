To The One Who Never Knew...

This story is for you—
not because you stayed,
but because you changed me.
For every time I smiled at your reply,
for every time your silence broke me,
for every time I wrote instead of cried—
Thank you.
You may never understand what you meant to me,
but in every chapter, every verse,
you exist.
Even if the world forgets,
I’ll always remember us—
the version that never got to be.

- Yours, silently
YG Bikram
